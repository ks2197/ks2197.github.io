Following are the projects on which i have worked :

Soundless sensors

I am developing an algorithm that employs linear regression to analyze all auxiliary channels potentially containing information about noise sources in LIGO. The aim is to identify a small, useful subset for reconstructing variations in LIGO’s astrophysical range.

Proposed Parallel Computing Algorithm streamlines the calculation of Spearman and Pearson correlation coefficients between each auxiliary channel and the range. It then generates a webpage that ranks the channels based on their highest overall correlation coefficients.

Overall it also possess the capability to identify groups of channels related to LIGO’s sensitivity fluctuations. This identification and elimination of noise sources restrict LIGO’s range thereby enabling more effective signal-to-noise observations and increases the number of observations.


MoodTunes
Developed a music recommendation application that utilizes deep learning to analyze user’s brainwave signals (EEG) and recommend music that aligns with their emotional state.

Recorded emotional responses using open BCI software and employed a deep Learning model for categorizing them into three groups: antagonistic, impartial, and favorable.

The application suggests and plays songs that correspond to the user’s present emotional state, thus delivering a customized and emotionally resonant music experience.

Cred Bot

Developed an automatic tool using Python that functions as a Credit Score Monitoring service.

Capable of analyzing available credit score of user to identify trends and correlations between credit scores and other factors such as income, education, and location.

Offers a wide range of features, including predicting future credit scores based on user financial history and behavior, as well as comparing credit scores with others in their own group or region to gain insights into their relative financial health.

CNN based model for classifying and detecting crop diseases

Developed a CNN Based model by implementing various layers such as CNN, Maxpooling, Dropout, and batch normalization in the model architecture to address feature extraction, spatial downsampling, overfitting, and to stabilize and expedite the training process.

Rigorously tested the mechanism using a real-time dataset consisting of images of infected crop leaves and corresponding disease labels obtained from various paddy fields in Tamil Nadu. The model was successfull in detecting the diseases, contributing to the advancement of agricultural disease management.
